About Musikfest



Our Mission


The mission of Musikfest is to provide the finest musical education programs and orchestras to aspiring young musicians. Our programs are designed for dedicated wind and string players between the age of 12-18. We are based in Northern Virginia and our rehearsals happen mainly in McLean, VA

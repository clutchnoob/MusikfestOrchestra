About Musikfest


Musikfest Ensemble provides the finest musical educational programs in the Northern Virginia area. We have been rated Superior by the National Federation of Music Clubs (NFMC) and the Virginia Federation of Music Clubs (VFMC). Musikfest is designed for committed string and wind players ages 12-18 years old with rehearsals on Saturday mornings in McLean, Virginia...
